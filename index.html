Why to join us?

Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW). The event is back for its FIFTH edition where it aims to be a platform for participants to test their engineering skills and showcase their prowess in the VIRTUAL mode.
